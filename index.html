About Orion 2019

Thapar Institute's original Technical Fest is finally here. Excited?

Orion has emerged as TIET's own Science and Technology Festival, an amalgamation of scientific thinking and innovation. It has been built with an aim to enhance the technical culture and to empower the technological spirit in the campus. Orion is a symbol of competitive spirit, achievement and pride. In this journey, you will find different ways to solve several industrial challenges, and encounter unique competition along with some motivating orations, state of the art technology and breathtaking performances.
